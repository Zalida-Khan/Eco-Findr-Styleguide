Logo

Vertical Logo

Our logo embodies the essence of exploration and discovery within our web app, symbolized by the magnifying glass. This iconic representation signifies our commitment to facilitating the search and discovery of events both locally and across the globe. Moreover, the inclusion of the earth also underscores our dedication to environmental sustainability. By highlighting events that contribute to a greener world, we aim to inspire collective action towards environmental conservation and stewardship. The leaves sprouting from the magnifying glass symbolize not only growth and opportunity but also the flourishing potential inherent in embracing eco-conscious practices. Our logo represents more than just an imageâ€”it embodies our vision of promoting exploration, global connections, and environmental responsibility through the variety of events on our platform.

Horizontal Logo

The green and blue colors in our logo represent our focus on both environmental sustainability and global connectivity. Green stands for eco-friendliness and growth, reflecting our commitment to promoting sustainable practices. Blue signifies the diverse range of events worldwide and emphasizes our aim to facilitate connections across the globe. These colors together represent our dedication to fostering a sustainable future while encouraging exploration and collaboration globally.

Font

Wendy One

We chose the Wendy One font for our Eco Findr app logo because it adds a fun and playful touch that fits our brand. This font's lively style matches our goal of making event discovery enjoyable for users. With Wendy One, our logo reflects the excitement and fun users can expect when exploring eco-friendly events through our app.
