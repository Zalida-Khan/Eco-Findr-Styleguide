Typography

Figtree

Eco Findr uses the Figtree font throughout the app. Figtree is a clean yet friendly geometric sans serif font, that includes a full set of weights, ranging from Extra Light to Black. The font has a hight x-height to maximize legibility and is a nice minimal and versatile font for UI design. This font was chosen for its ease of readability and balanced look, it has a compact, and a rounder build.
