Accessibility

Added to Favorites

When users add an event to their favorites, a pop-up notification appears accompanied by a checkmark icon beside the words. This visual confirmation enhances user experience by providing immediate feedback on their action.

Event Card Photos

We included event photos in our web app to enhance accessibility and engagement. These visuals offer users a more immersive experience, aiding understanding and connection with the events. With descriptive captions, they cater to users with visual or cognitive impairments, ensuring inclusivity and a richer experience for all

In our web app, we've focused on consistent navigation labels to help users navigate effortlessly. This approach ensures users can explore, find information, and use app features with ease. Additionally, we've included a tutorial page for users who may need extra guidance.

Colour Accessibility

Colour Palette

We carefully selected a color palette for our web app, including dark green, other shades of green, and neutral colors, with accessibility in mind. Dark green signifies depth and vitality, while other shades of green evoke growth and harmony with nature. Neutral colors provide balance and enhance readability. By using a combination of these colors, we ensure that the app's interface is visually appealing and easy to navigate for all users, including those with visual impairments or color blindness. We also made sure there's enough contrast between text and background colors for better visibility. This approach promotes inclusivity and a positive user experience for all.

Contrast Ratio: 12.05

The deep green background creates a strong visual impact. The white text(#FFF) stands out clearly against the dark background, ensuring good readability. This combination meets accessibility guidelines, providing sufficient contrast for most users.

Contrast Ratio: 5.73

The medium green background offers a calm and balanced aesthetic. However, the contrast ratio with white text(#FFF) is lower, which may affect readability for some users, especially those with visual impairments. While it doesn't fully meet accessibility guidelines, it may still be acceptable in certain contexts.

Contrast Ratio: 10.31

The light green background conveys a sense of hope and vitality. The black text(#000) contrasts well against the light background, ensuring clear readability. This combination meets accessibility guidelines, providing adequate contrast for most users.

Contrast Ratio: 16.25

The pale green background creates a serene and peaceful atmosphere. The black text (#000) stands out prominently against the light background, ensuring excellent readability. This combination exceeds accessibility guidelines, offering high contrast suitable for all users.

Contrast Ratio: 18.31

The light yellow background evokes warmth and positivity. The black text(#000) contrasts sharply against the bright background, ensuring optimal readability. This combination greatly exceeds accessibility guidelines, providing exceptional contrast for all users.
